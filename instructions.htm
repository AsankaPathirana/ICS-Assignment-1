Instructions for using Group of Fans

About deferent controllers

Individual Controller

Fan uses the ON/OFF button to power on/off and theReverse button to reverse the rotating direction.
The Slider bar uses to control the speed of the fan.
Group Controller

Individual Fan Controllers are over write by the group controller.
Group Fan controller uses the ON/OFF button to power on/off of the entire Fans and theReverse button to reverse the rotating direction of the entire Fans .

The Slider bar uses to control the speed of the entire Fans.

The number of fans available can be changed. Enter the fans count in the given Text Fieldand hit APPLY button.
